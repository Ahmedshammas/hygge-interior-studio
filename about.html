About Us

Crafting personal and functional spaces.

Principal Architect & Founder

Our Philosophy

At Hygge Interior Studio, we believe in designing not just spaces, but emotions — spaces that feel warm, functional, and truly personal. Our design philosophy is rooted in the Danish concept of "Hygge" – a quality of coziness and comfortable conviviality that engenders a feeling of contentment or well-being.

We are a Chennai-based studio founded by Farid Ar, specializing in bespoke, minimal, and cozy interiors that elevate everyday living. We handle everything from initial concept to final execution, ensuring a seamless journey for our clients, whether it's a single room refresh or a full-scale renovation.

Our Approach

  • Collaborate: We listen to your story and your needs to co-create a vision.
  • Design: We blend form and function, creating beautiful, livable spaces.
  • Execute: We manage the entire process with precision and care, from start to finish.
